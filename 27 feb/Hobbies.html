My Hobbies & Interest:

Travel

Curiosity, courage, and self-organization. Traveling can prove you are not afraid of stepping outside your comfort zone and learning new things. And flexibility and adaptability are among the most important workplace personal traits.

Sports

It does not matter what sport you do. Exercising in general develops self-discipline, patience, and helps bounce back from disappointment. Team sports create opportunities to improve leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, too.

Yoga

You might say yoga is a type of sport, but it is much more than stretching. It also involves concentrating on breathing and is a great way to relax your mind. And data shows a relaxed employee is less likely to leave their job.

Dance

Dancing is not just fun. It is a social activity that teaches collaboration skills. It also boosts cognitive performance and helps to unwind.
